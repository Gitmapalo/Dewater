Dewatering Methods

Three core approaches to manage water effectively in open pit operations

Pumping Systems

High-capacity submersible and surface pumps designed for continuous water removal from active mining zones.

  • • Submersible deep well systems
  • • Surface centrifugal pumps
  • • Well point arrays
  • • Automated control systems

Drainage Channels

Engineered surface drainage networks that redirect water flow away from critical operational areas.

  • • Perimeter ditch systems
  • • Collection sumps & basins
  • • Cascade channel design
  • • Sediment control features

Groundwater Control

Subsurface intervention techniques to manage aquifer levels and prevent water ingress into the pit.

  • • Deep well dewatering
  • • Pressure relief systems
  • • Cutoff wall barriers
  • • Recharge & injection wells
